Each element MUST be an object identified by `chunk_index` (the chunk index), in ONE of two forms: to narrow a large chunk to the relevant parts, use {"chunk_index": <index>, "lines": [[start, end], ...]} where `lines` are absolute line-number ranges to keep from that chunk; to keep an entire chunk (small chunks, or chunks that are wholly relevant), use {"chunk_index": <index>, "keep": "full"}. Only include chunks that are actually relevant to the query.
